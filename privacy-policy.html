Privacy Policy — Browsing Timer

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

Browsing Timer is a browser extension that tracks your browsing time per website. Your privacy is our top priority. This extension is designed to work entirely offline with zero data collection.

Data Collection

We do not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data or browsing data.

Specifically, this extension:

  • Does not collect any personally identifiable information
  • Does not send any data to external servers
  • Does not use analytics, tracking pixels, or telemetry
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not make any network requests
  • Does not use remote code

Data Storage

All browsing time data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API. This data never leaves your browser. Data older than 30 days is automatically deleted.

Permissions Used

  • tabs — To detect the active tab and read the current page URL for time tracking
  • idle — To pause tracking when you are inactive
  • storage — To save browsing time data locally on your device
  • alarms — To periodically save data and clean up old records
  • webNavigation — To count website visits

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
